Introducing our custom ESP32-C6 development board, designed to streamline your prototyping with built-in components that reduce wiring complexity and maximize functionality. With WiFi 6, BLE, and USB-C programming, this board includes a high-power voltage regulator, dual motor drivers/high power output pins, integrated addressable LEDs, and a Qwiic/Stemma QT port for quick sensor and module connections. Ideal for robotics, IoT, and smart device projects, this board empowers you to jumpstart development with powerful, versatile hardware and minimal wiring overhead.

Step 1

This is a one-time setup. Open Arduino IDE and go to Settings/Preferences.

Step 2

In Additional Board Manager URLs, paste: ESP32 board URL

Step 3

Go to Tools > Board > Board Manager.

Step 4

In the search box, type esp32 and install Espressif Systems.

Step 5

Connect your board, select ESP32C6 Dev Module, and choose the port.

Our board uses a USB-C connector for both data transfer and power, offering a modern, reversible port that ensures quick and easy programming access with any USB-C cable.

Designed to support a range of power inputs, this board can run on 5V through the USB-C port or any external 5-16V power supply for flexible applications.

The built-in 5V, 3A BEC provides stable power, ideal for motors and other high-power components, ensuring seamless performance in demanding projects.

Integrated motor drivers simplify robotics projects, allowing for dual motor control with high-power output pins directly from the board.

Offering a versatile array of GPIO pins, this board supports digital I/O, analog input, PWM, and more, for wide compatibility with various components and sensors.

The onboard Qwiic connector supports I2C modules from SparkFun and Adafruit, making it easy to expand with additional sensors and peripherals.

Built-in WS2812B addressable LEDs offer customizable lighting options, perfect for adding visual feedback or aesthetic appeal to your projects.

With WiFi 6 capability, this board ensures high-speed, reliable wireless connectivity, even in dense network environments.

Featuring BLE 5.0 support, this board is ready for low-power, high-efficiency Bluetooth applications, ideal for IoT and smart devices.
